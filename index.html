Summary: SecureScan is a web vulnerability scanner app. We prioritize your privacy and collect minimal data necessary to provide our service.

1. Information We Collect

SecureScan collects the following information:

  • URLs you scan: The website addresses you submit for vulnerability scanning
  • Host addresses: IP addresses or domains you submit for port scanning
  • Scan results: The vulnerability scan results generated for your submitted URLs

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or other personal data

2. How We Use Your Information

The URLs and hosts you submit are used solely to:

  • Perform the vulnerability scan you requested
  • Display scan results to you within the app

We do not store scan data permanently. Scan results are generated in real-time and are not retained on our servers after your session.

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. The only external connections made are:

  • To the URLs you submit for scanning (to test for vulnerabilities)
  • To our API server to process scan requests

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all API communications
  • No permanent storage of scan data
  • No user accounts or authentication required

5. Children's Privacy

SecureScan is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can use SecureScan anonymously.
